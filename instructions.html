Instructions for Crop Recommendation System

Steps to Follow:

  1. Enter Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium values of your soil: These are essential nutrients that play a crucial role in crop growth. Accurate values are important for a correct recommendation.
  2. Enter the pH of your soil: The pH level of your soil determines its acidity or alkalinity, which is vital for crop health.
  3. Enter the Temperature, Humidity, and Rainfall of your location: These environmental factors greatly affect crop growth. Ensure that you enter the current or average data for your location.
  4. How to Measure Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, and pH: You can measure these values using specialized devices or by sending a soil sample to a laboratory for testing. This will give you accurate readings for these essential soil components.

Important Notes:

  • Ensure that all the fields are filled out accurately to get the best recommendation.
  • The system will use the entered values to recommend the most suitable crop for your soil and environmental conditions.
  • If you are unsure about the values for Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, or pH, consult with local agricultural experts or use soil testing kits.

How to Get the Crop Recommendation:

Once you’ve filled out the form with the required data, click the 'Get Recommendation' button. The system will analyze the data and provide you with a crop recommendation based on the conditions you’ve entered.
